Tip: how to use bookmarklets

Info: this site does not support bookmarkletsgo due to Content Security Policy.

  • Just click title of bookmarklets.
  • Drag and drop bookmarklets above to the 'Bookmarks Bar' of your browser then click from the 'Bookmarks Bar'.
  • Just bookmark current URL, then edit bookmark, delete from 'http' to '#'.
  • Click 'Copy' button, then add a new bookmark with copied URL.
